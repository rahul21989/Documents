AlphaTrace – Support

AlphaTrace is an educational app that helps children learn to trace letters of the alphabet.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is AlphaTrace designed for?
AlphaTrace is designed for children aged 3–8 years old.

Does the app require an internet connection?
No. AlphaTrace works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

Does the app collect any data?
No. AlphaTrace does not collect any personal data, analytics, or device information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does the app contain ads?
No. AlphaTrace is completely ad-free.
