Kids Kala – Support

Kids Kala is an educational app that helps children learn and explore creativity through drawing and coloring activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is Kids Kala designed for?
Kids Kala is designed for children aged 3–8 years old.

Does the app require an internet connection?
No. Kids Kala works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

Does the app collect any data?
No. Kids Kala does not collect any personal data, analytics, or device information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does the app contain ads?
No. Kids Kala is completely ad-free.
