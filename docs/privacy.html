Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AlphaTrace ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

Information We Collect

AlphaTrace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This includes:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

AlphaTrace is designed for children under 13 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app functions entirely offline and requires no account, login, or personal details of any kind.

Third-Party Services

AlphaTrace does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data-sharing services.

Data Storage

All app data (tracing progress, settings) is stored locally on the user's device only and is never transmitted externally.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
